About the game

Welcome to Zombie Harvest... This FPS game sets the stage for a grand narrative (Chapter 1).


In this game, you will have to survive a full night in a forest facing multiple waves of zombies.


You play as an unknown man who came to a deserted island for a weekend retreat... or so he thought. On the afternoon before his planned departure, a thick fog began to rise, preventing him from continuing to pack his belongings. He soon realized that the island was actually occupied by zombies.


In the fog, something was approaching him, and it took him a moment to understand that the shadow moving towards him was not human!


The rest of the story is for you to discover...
